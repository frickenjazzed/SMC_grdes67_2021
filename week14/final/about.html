Our Mission

While we have the tools to allow adults to self-treat, the thousands of children libing with type 1 diabetes are solely dependant on other. When children can't hold the insulin pen or remember previous injection sites, caretakes and parents' help is crucial to their survival.

We aim to give the power and control to the kids while also making this chronic disease less daunting and more empowering for the child.

How Fusible Inc Started

When Donna's son was diagnosed with T1 Diabetes at age 6, she felt at a loss with how to make him feel less frustrated. The rounds of insulin and remembering injection sites at school and having to rely on the school nurse ended in more tears than she liked. So she set out to find a way to make it easier.

It all started by finding out that there is a huge field of research into using temporary tattoo to help diabetics and other medical condition. Donna combined what she learned from their research and what would help her son better control his own medical condition.

Thus Fusible Inc was born.

About Donna

Donna is the mother of 2 young ones and founder of Fusible Inc. She counts herself lucky to be able to give her child this kind of help and works to bring Fusible Inc to kids around the world.

Donna's big goal is to raise enough awareness and money to donate Fusible Inc tattoos to T1 diabetic children in third world countries.
