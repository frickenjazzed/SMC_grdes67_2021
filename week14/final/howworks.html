How it Works

No one should have to feel left out or isolated just because they need medication. Fusible Inc makes it fun for kids (or anyone!) to take their insulin shot every day.

How to apply

Peel away the plastic
Place tattoo and apply with damp cloth
Once the tattoo is on the body
Pinch to inject into the fat
Position pen and inject
Once you've used a dot, use alcohol to disinfect and remove the colored dot
Remove the colored dot after each injection
After removing the last colored dot, use alcohol to remove the tattoo

How to remove

There are 3 different ways to remove your Fusible Inc

1. Place a piece of Scotch tape over the tattoo, rub it gently and peel off
2. Apply rubbing alcohol,hand sanitizer, wait 10 seconds and gently rub off with a cloth
3. Apply baby lotion/baby oil, wait 10 seconds and gently rub off with a cloth
A wet cloth/ sponge can be used to gently rub off whatever is left
