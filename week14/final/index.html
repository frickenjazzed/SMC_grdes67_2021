Temporary Tattoos for Injection Rotations

No one should have to feel left out or isolated just because they need medication. Fusible Inc makes it fun for kids (or anyone!) to take their insulin shot every day.

How it Works

Combining Tattoos and Medical Care

We believe that when children with chronic conditions take responsibility for their own medicines, injections, and other activities to manage their condition, they acheieve a sense of independence and security, which is reflected in their quality of life and confidence in themselves.

Many designs

Designs for the arm, stomach, and leg and different images to chose from. There's something for everyone!

Bright colors

Bright colors show kids exactly where to aim to give them the most control over their own injections.

Not just for kids

Fusible Inc isn't just for kids! Adults enjoy the awesone designs and ease of use, too.

How it works

Peel protective plastic
Apply to the chosen area
Pinch the skin to inject into the fat
Remove the used dots with alcohol wipe
Remove tattoo when all the dots are gone
