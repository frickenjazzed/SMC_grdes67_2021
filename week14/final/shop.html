About Donna

Donna is the mother of 2 young ones and founder of Fusible Inc. She counts herself lucky to be able to give her child this kind of help and works to bring Fusible Inc to kids around the world.

Donna's big goal is to raise enough awareness and money to donate Fusible Inc tattoos to T1 diabetic children in third world countries.
